## Corpus Provenance

The benchmark input is classified as:

~~~text
corpus_kind=replicated_real_payload
unique_continuous_drive=false
physical_record_order_preserved=true
logical_segment_ranges_non_overlapping=true
~~~

The corpus was generated by
`tools/build_mcap_benchmark_corpus.py`.

The builder:

- reads actual encoded MCAP message payloads without decoding them;
- preserves the common schema and channel contract;
- remaps schemas and channels into one output MCAP;
- preserves each source segment's physical message order;
- shifts segment timestamps into non-overlapping logical ranges;
- cycles through source scenes until the requested physical size
  has been reached;
- records corpus and segment provenance as MCAP metadata.

The corpus must not be described as 10 GiB of unique continuous
driving data.

It is a size-targeted performance corpus composed from repeated
actual nuScenes-derived payloads.
