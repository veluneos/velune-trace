Velune Trace runs locally and performs no telemetry or automatic raw-log upload. It structures observed evidence and differences, but does not automatically determine root cause, fault, liability, safety, or regression.

Stop searching the entire log.

Large robotics logs force engineers to manually inspect topics, timestamps, gaps, jitter, commands, and motion. VeluneOS narrows the investigation surface and produces reproducible evidence reports for human review.

Local-first

Process MCAP and ROS2-derived evidence without requiring centralized raw-log upload.

Reference comparison

Compare one explicit Target against every user-selected private Reference.

Reproducible handoff

Preserve JSON as the source of truth with a bounded Markdown summary for engineering review.
